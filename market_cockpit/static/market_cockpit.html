Formula summary

  • Latest return: current close divided by the previous open-session close, minus one.
  • A zero-volume and zero-amount row is no-trade and is excluded from breadth, participation, rolling close, and risk windows.
  • 20/60-session metrics require every close in the full persisted calendar window.
  • Participation compares the current value with the median of the prior 20 sessions.
  • Liquidity distribution uses finite, strictly positive amount observations on the existing effective session. Top-decile membership is max(1, ceil(10% * eligible count)), with amount-descending and stock-code-ascending tie order.
  • Liquidity activity uses one fixed matched cohort with eligible amount on the effective session and every exact prior 5 or 20 open sessions. The ratio divides the cohort's current total by the median of its prior session totals.
  • Above-baseline liquidity uses the exact 20-prior-session matched cohort and a strict greater-than comparison against each stock's own prior median.
  • Price-behavior momentum requires every valid traded close in exact 21- or 61-open-session windows; intermediate gaps invalidate the stock even when both endpoints exist.
  • Per-stock 20-return volatility uses all 20 close-to-close returns, sample standard deviation with ddof=1, and annualization by sqrt(252).
  • The price-behavior quadrant uses one fixed cohort eligible for return-20, return-60, and volatility-20. It splits 60-session momentum at zero and volatility at the matched median; ties remain on the lower-or-equal side.
  • Risk uses 20 complete selected-universe equal-weight returns and annualizes volatility by square root of 252.
  • Benchmark context uses its own explicit series and the selected equity snapshot's persisted open-session calendar. Return, SMA, volatility, and drawdown require exact consecutive 2/20/60/21-session windows ending at each code's latest valid session.
  • Sector context uses exact Eastmoney BK identifiers and separate complete snapshots. Latest, 5-session, 20-session, SMA20, volatility, and drawdown metrics require exact 2/6/21/20/21-session windows.
  • Sector top/bottom lists are descriptive selected-scope observations. Null metrics are excluded from denominators and ties use the stable sector code.

Formula references: docs/market_cockpit.md, docs/benchmark_context.md, and docs/sector_context.md
