Formula summary

  • Latest return: current close divided by the previous open-session close, minus one.
  • A zero-volume and zero-amount row is no-trade and is excluded from breadth, participation, rolling close, and risk windows.
  • 20/60-session metrics require every close in the full persisted calendar window.
  • Participation compares the current value with the median of the prior 20 sessions.
  • Risk uses 20 complete selected-universe equal-weight returns and annualizes volatility by square root of 252.
